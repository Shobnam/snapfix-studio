How to Use SnapFix Studio

SnapFix Studio is designed to make your image editing simple and efficient. Follow these steps to get started:

  1. Upload your image: Click the "Choose Image File" button and select the image you want to edit.
  2. Select an action: Choose the desired operation from the dropdown:
    • No Conversion (PNG)
    • Convert to Grayscale
    • Convert to JPG
    • Convert to WEBP
    • Convert to PDF
  3. Optional - Flip your image: Select horizontal or vertical flip if you want to mirror your image.
  4. Optional - Resize: Enter width and height in pixels to resize your image.
  5. Optional - Target Size: For JPG or WEBP, specify a target file size (in KB) to compress your image.
  6. Submit: Click the "Edit Image" button. Your edited image will be processed and downloaded automatically.

If you have any questions or need help, feel free to contact us.
