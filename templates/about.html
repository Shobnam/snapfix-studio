About SnapFix Studio

Welcome to SnapFix Studio — a versatile web tool designed to help you manage and transform your images with ease.

Features:

  • Convert RGB images to Grayscale: Quickly turn your color images into classic black & white.
  • Image format conversion: Convert your images into PDF format for easy sharing and printing.
  • Resize images: Resize PNG, JPG, and WEBP images to any custom dimensions you need.
  • Flip images: Flip images horizontally or vertically for creative effects.

Our application uses the power of Python and OpenCV to provide reliable and efficient image processing capabilities, all accessible through your browser.

Whether you're preparing images for a presentation, website, or personal project, SnapFix Studio makes the process smooth and straightforward.
